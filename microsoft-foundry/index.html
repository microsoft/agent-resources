Advanced fine-tuning techniques

  • Reinforcement fine-tuning (RFT) — train with custom verifiers and reward signals; generally available with o4-mini
  • Model distillation — transfer reasoning from a large teacher model into a smaller, faster student model
  • Stored completions — capture production outputs for distillation and evaluation datasets
  • Synthetic data generation for fine-tuning — generate and curate training data with AI assistance
